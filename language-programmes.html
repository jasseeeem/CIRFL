    Language Programmes and Other Courses

  • A certificate course in the Japanese language in NIT Calicut. The certificate course will start on October 3 (tentatively), and the course is designed as a 93 hours instruction-based contact program focusing on Katsudo and Rikai for registered participants. After completing the program, participants will receive a certificate from Japan Foundation, New Delhi and NIT Calicut in collaboration. Seats are limited and will be offered on a first-come, first-served.
